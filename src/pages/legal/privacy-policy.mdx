## The short version

PSL/i is built so that knowing you never means losing control of your data. You can see everything PSL/i holds, where each fact came from, and export or delete all of it at any time. With the Home Server tier, your data never leaves your house.

## What we collect

Only what you connect. PSL/i resolves meaning from the apps you explicitly link — email, calendar, contacts, messaging, documents, and notes. Your connected apps remain the system of record; PSL/i holds a resolved, source-traced understanding on top of them.

## How your data is used

Your resolved meaning powers your lenses and Navigator. Every write is human-gated — PSL/i proposes, and nothing is sent or changed until you approve.

## Your controls

- **See everything.** Every fact carries a source trace.
- **Export everything**, anytime, without a support ticket.
- **Delete everything**, anytime. Your Foundation belongs to you.

## Home Server

If you run PSL/i on the Home Server appliance, your Foundation stays physically in your home and the encryption keys stay with the device.
